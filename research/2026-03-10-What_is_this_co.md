Tactix in Australia primarily refers to Tactix Group, an infrastructure consultancy that specializes in the transport and infrastructure sectors, offering services in project execution and resource management. The company, which has operated from its Sydney head office since 2012, focuses on strategic project development, construction, railway signaling and telecoms, and mechanical and electrical systems.

Tactix Group was recently acquired by Sener, a large Spanish engineering and technology firm. The agreement for Sener to acquire a 60% stake in Tactix Group was signed in 2022, with the remaining 40% staying with the original Australian shareholders. Following the acquisition, the company now operates under the name Tactix-Sener. The new entity officially opened its Australian Headquarters in Sydney on November 8, 2023. This acquisition aims to expand Sener's presence in the Australian transport sector and enable Tactix-Sener to undertake larger projects and offer a broader range of services.

It is worth noting that other entities in Australia also use the "Tactix" name, including a brand for durable tools, storage, and accessories, and Tactix Services, which provides personalized training, support, and supported independent living in the Hunter, Newcastle, and Lake Macquarie regions. However, the recent acquisition specifically pertains to Tactix Group, the infrastructure consultancy.
